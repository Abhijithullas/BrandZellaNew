About our agency

At Brand Zella, our journey is marked by notable achievements and milestones that reflect our commitment to excellence. Over the years, we've proudly participated in numerous prestigious fashion shows, where our models and designs have left an indelible mark.

  • Summer Fashion Camp Triumph: A defining moment in our journey was our participation in the Summer Fashion Camp, where our models stood out among the competition.
  • Best Model Recognition: Brand Zella proudly clinched the first prize for Best Model, a testament to the exceptional talent we nurture and represent.
  • Best Designer of the Show Award: Adding another feather to our cap, Brand Zella was honored with the prestigious Best Designer of the Show award, highlighting our agency's commitment to innovation and creativity.
  • Best Iconic Award: Brand Zella has also been bestowed with the esteemed Best Iconic Award, a recognition of our agency's ability to create and represent iconic styles and concepts in the world of fashion and advertising.
